ftp:// cookies test

This is a manual test procedure:

  • Click on this link to open a new window.
  • Type javascript:document.cookie="HELLOWORLD=1;path=/";alert(document.cookie); in the URL bar of that window.
  • If no pop-up dialog comes, or an empty one appears, ftp:// cookies are not supported. In such a case, stop here. Otherwise, ftp:// cookies work.
  • Otherwise, click here to open a new page in that window.
  • Type javascript:alert(document.cookie) in that window. If a dialog box with "HELLOWORLD" comes up, ftp:// cookies are not context-separated from http:// cookies (otherwise, they are).
